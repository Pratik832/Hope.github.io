About Us

About the Company:

Hope is your go to platform enabling you to access immediate and efficient mental health care. Your wellness coach is just a quick 3 clicks away. We are your partners in your wellness journey and we're here to guide you along the way,into a future with better and stronger mental health.

Vision:

To create a society where everyone is open to seeking mental health help and care. There's no stigma, barriers, boundaries or judgement. Seeking mental health help and care is as easy and accessible as every other healthcare need.

Mission:

Hope is here to help you receive immediate and efficient mental health care. We aim to break down the barriers of geography and inaccessibility by connecting you to our wellness coaches and community, who will be your strength and support away from home.

Mental health facts!

Common signs of mental health issues include: extreme mood swings,excessive worrying or fear,and avoiding friends or social activities.

Youth depression rates have risen from 5.9% to 8.2% since 2012. Depression symptoms can impact performance in school and interfere with personal relationships.

Depression is the leading cause of disability worldwide.

More than 1 in 4 adults living with serious mental illnesses also struggles with substance abuse.

About half of mental disorders begin before the age of 14.

Almost 800 000 people die by suicide every year; 1 person dies from suicide every 40 seconds. Suicide is the second leading cause of death in individuals aged 15-29 years.

Around 1 in 9 people in settings affected by conflict have a moderate or severe mental disorder.

People with severe mental disorders die 10 to 20 years earlier than the general population.
